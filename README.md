# LeafAngleSurveyData
Compiled data of survey species for warming and elevated carbon dioxide induced hyponasty.

excel spreadsheet containing the data collected from several small survey experiments examining how leaf morphology is affected by the combination of chronic warming and elevated carbon dioxide. Data collected by Michael Thomas of the Univeristy of Toledo Scott Heckathorn lab. Funded by United States Department of Agriculture.

SurveyMetaData contains descriptions of data collected.
